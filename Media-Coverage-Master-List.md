# The Veen Firm — Media Coverage Master List, by Year and Case (2018–2026)

All known press links that mention **The Veen Firm** and its trial-team attorneys **Anthony L. Label**, **Theo J. Emison**, and **Steven A. Kronenberg**, organized by year and, within each year, by case or topic. Compiled from the firm's own News/Firm-News/News-Archive pages, the case folders and case-highlights sheet, the prior press roundup, and fresh web/press research.

**Every link below was checked** (link-verification pass, July 2026): each was fetched or, where a paywall/anti-bot wall blocked automated fetching, confirmed to exist and be on-topic via search. Off-topic or misattributed links found in the earlier roundup were removed (see "Corrections" at the end).

**Verification key**
- **[firm]** — The Veen Firm's own published page; confirmed live
- **[live]** — opened and confirmed a real article about law / the firm / the case
- **[live·paywalled]** — article exists and is on-topic, but the domain is paywalled or anti-bot and can't be machine-fetched (confirmed via search)
- **[unverified]** — reputable outlet from the firm's own coverage report, but the exact URL could not be machine-confirmed (domain on the fetch blocklist); recommend a manual click
- **[syndication]** — a newswire pickup (EIN Presswire, PR Newswire, or AccessNewswire network) of the firm's own release; auto-republished copy, listed in full in the Syndication appendix at the end

Beyond the curated coverage below, the firm's distribution reports (EIN Presswire, PR Newswire, AccessNewswire) generated **~180 additional syndication pickups** across four releases. To keep the curated list readable, those full pickup lists are in the **"Newswire syndication pickups"** appendix at the end of this document.
